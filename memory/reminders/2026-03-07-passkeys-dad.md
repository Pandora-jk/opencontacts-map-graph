**Topic:** Passkeys - Simple Explanation

## Quick Summary for Jim

**What are passkeys?**
- Password replacements that use your phone/computer's biometrics (fingerprint, face scan) or screen lock
- Can't be phished or stolen like passwords
- Work across all your devices automatically

**Key points to cover:**
1. **No more passwords to remember** - uses device unlock instead
2. **Can't be hacked remotely** - private key stays on your device
3. **Auto-fills on trusted devices** - no typing codes
4. **Works everywhere** - Google, Apple, Microsoft, most major sites now support it

**Analogy:** Like having a bouncer (your phone) that knows you personally instead of carrying a paper list of passwords that anyone could copy.

**Why it matters:** Eliminates 99% of account takeover attacks. Easier AND more secure.
