# Research Report Outline Template

Use this when a report card requires an outline that is ready for drafting and pricing.

## Working Title
- Clear promise
- Narrow audience
- Specific problem or decision

## Target Reader
- Who buys or uses the report
- What they are already trying to do
- Why they need the report now

## Commercial Promise
- What the report helps the reader decide, fix, or capture
- What outcome the reader should expect after reading

## Core Thesis
- One sentence argument
- Why this argument is defensible
- What the report rejects

## Report Structure
- Executive summary
- Scoring framework
- Chapter-by-chapter outline
- Final recommendation

## Evidence Inventory
- Existing workspace files
- Data or examples already available
- Gaps to fill during drafting

## Pricing and Packaging
- Launch price
- Upsell or bundle
- CTA after purchase

## Drafting Plan
- What to draft first
- What to validate before publishing
- What supporting assets to add later
