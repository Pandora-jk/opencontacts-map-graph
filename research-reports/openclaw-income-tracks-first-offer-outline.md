# OpenClaw Income Tracks: What Still Works After the Hype

## Working Title
- Primary title: `OpenClaw Income Tracks: What Still Works After the Hype`
- Subtitle: `A decision report for solo operators choosing between services, micro-products, data packs, and automation-driven offers`
- Positioning: practical market map, not generic AI-business inspiration

## Target Reader
- Solo operators and small AI builders who already tried scattered income experiments
- Buyers who want one defensible lane instead of juggling seven half-built tracks
- Existing OpenClaw users who need a commercial direction, not more setup advice

## Commercial Promise
- Help the reader rank the current workspace income tracks by speed to cash, durability, and defensible edge
- Show why service-led outbound work wins before productization
- Give the reader a concrete "focus now / incubate later / pause" decision table

## Core Thesis
- Sustainable OpenClaw revenue does not come from generic autonomy alone; it comes from a defensible edge plus a distribution path that can reach paying buyers quickly.
- The strongest first lane is a service-backed offer for founder-led B2B agencies, with GitHub bounties and tightly scoped products treated as secondary support lanes.
- The report should explicitly reject hype-driven trading, broad "AI product" speculation, and undifferentiated data-pack offers as primary bets.

## Report Structure
1. Executive summary: one-page recommendation and the winning lane
2. Decision framework: capital required, time to first dollar, defensibility, repeatability, compliance burden, and delivery complexity
3. Track review: GitHub portfolio and bounties
4. Track review: micro-product automation scripts
5. Track review: data brokerage and niche lead packs
6. Track review: done-for-you outbound pipeline services
7. Track review: templates, affiliate plays, and lightweight upsells
8. Final operating model: focus on one active lane, incubate one secondary lane, pause everything else
9. Appendix: offer snapshots, risk table, and next-14-day execution plan
