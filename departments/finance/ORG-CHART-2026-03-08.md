## Live Department Structure

### 1. Finance Head
- Owns portfolio decisions
- Enforces the max-two-active-lanes rule
- Kills or pauses weak ideas quickly

### 2. Revenue Service Owner
- Owns the B2B pipeline setup service
- Tracks outreach, replies, demos, closes, and delivery quality
- Holds the first proof-of-demand mandate

### 3. Legal Expert
- Reviews outreach and complaint handling
- Maintains suppression and unsubscribe process
- Blocks unsafe execution

### 4. GitHub Bounties Owner
- Screens narrow technical bounties
- Runs only as secondary cash
- Must not slow the core service

### 5. Website Upsell Lane
- Incubating only
- Activated only when it helps sell or retain a pipeline client

## Paused Lanes

- Crypto
- Data brokerage
- Skills and templates as standalone products
- Standalone web design
- Generic AI product exploration

## Department Rule

If a lane does not support the core service or produce clear cash quickly, it stays paused.
