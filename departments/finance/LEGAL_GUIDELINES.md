## 1. Data Privacy & Protection

### 1.1 GDPR Compliance (EU)
**Applies to:** Any company processing data of EU residents or EU-based companies.

**Requirements:**
- ✅ **Lawful Basis:** Must have legitimate interest or consent for B2B data processing.
- ✅ **Data Minimization:** Only collect data necessary for lead qualification (company name, work email, job title).
- ✅ **Right to Access:** Must provide data subjects access to their data upon request.
- ✅ **Right to Erasure:** Must delete data upon request within 30 days.
- ✅ **Data Processing Agreement (DPA):** Required when processing customer data.

**Action Items:**
- [ ] Create Data Processing Agreement (DPA) template
- [ ] Implement data deletion workflow (automated)
- [ ] Add privacy policy to sales materials
- [ ] Document lawful basis: "Legitimate Interest" for B2B cold outreach (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f))

### 1.2 CCPA/CPRA Compliance (California, USA)
**Applies to:** Companies doing business with California residents.

**Requirements:**
- ✅ **Right to Know:** Disclose what personal information is collected.
- ✅ **Right to Delete:** Delete upon request.
- ✅ **No Sale of Data:** We do not sell data; we process it for customers.
- ✅ **Opt-Out Mechanism:** Provide way to opt-out of data processing.

**Action Items:**
- [ ] Add "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" link to privacy policy
- [ ] Implement opt-out tracking system

### 1.3 Data Sources
**Approved Sources (B2B, public data only):**
- ✅ Company websites (public contact pages)
- ✅ LinkedIn public profiles (work emails only)
- ✅ Business registries (ASIC, Companies House, etc.)
- ✅ Publicly available press releases/news

**Prohibited Sources:**
- ❌ Purchased email lists (no proof of consent)
- ❌ Scraped personal emails (Gmail, Yahoo, etc.)
- ❌ Data obtained through deception or hacking
- ❌ Any data marked "confidential" or "private"

## 2. Cold Email Compliance

### 2.1 CAN-SPAM Act (USA)
**Requirements:**
- ✅ **Accurate Headers:** No false "From" names or subjects.
- ✅ **Clear Identification:** Must identify as an advertisement/solicitation.
- ✅ **Physical Address:** Include valid postal address in every email.
- ✅ **Opt-Out Mechanism:** Clear, working unsubscribe link (process within 10 business days).
- ✅ **No Harvesting:** Don't scrape emails from websites that prohibit it.

**Compliance Checklist:**
- [ ] Email footer includes: "Pandora, [Physical Address], [Unsubscribe Link]"
- [ ] Subject lines are not misleading
- [ ] Unsubscribe requests processed within 10 days
- [ ] Maintain suppression list for opt-outs

### 2.2 GDPR (EU) - B2B Cold Email
**Requirements:**
- ✅ **Legitimate Interest:** Must demonstrate legitimate business interest.
- ✅ **Relevance:** Email must be relevant to recipient's role.
- ✅ **Opt-Out:** Clear opt-out mechanism in every email.
- ✅ **Data Minimization:** Only collect necessary data.

**Best Practice:**
- Target only business emails (info@, sales@, firstname.lastname@company.com)
- Avoid personal emails (gmail.com, yahoo.com, etc.)
- Limit to 1-2 follow-ups maximum

### 2.3 Australian Spam Act 2003
**Applies to:** Since operator is in Australia.

**Requirements:**
- ✅ **Consent:** Implied consent for B2B if relevant to role.
- ✅ **Identification:** Clear sender identity.
- ✅ **Unsubscribe:** Functional unsubscribe mechanism.

## 5. Risk Management

### 5.1 High-Risk Activities (AVOID)
- ❌ Sending emails to personal addresses (Gmail, Yahoo, etc.)
- ❌ Making false claims ("guaranteed 10x ROI" → use "customers report up to 10x ROI")
- ❌ Impersonation (do not pretend to be someone else)
- ❌ Harvesting emails from sites with "no scraping" policies
- ❌ Storing sensitive data (credit cards, passwords, health info)

### 5.2 Disclaimers (Required in Sales Materials)
- "ROI figures based on customer reports; individual results may vary."
- "Enrichment data sourced from public records; accuracy not guaranteed."
- "Service provided 'as-is' without warranty of specific outcomes."

### 5.3 Insurance (Recommended)
- Professional liability insurance ($1M coverage)
- Cyber liability insurance (data breach coverage)

## 7. Complaint Handling Process

**If a recipient complains (spam, privacy, etc.):**

1. **Immediate Action (Within 2 hours):**
   - Add to suppression list (no further emails)
   - Flag in customer's account

2. **Investigation (Within 24 hours):**
   - Review email content
   - Verify data source
   - Check consent basis

3. **Response (Within 48 hours):**
   - Apologize if error occurred
   - Confirm deletion of data (if requested)
   - Document incident

4. **Prevention:**
   - Update training data if systematic issue
   - Revise templates if needed
   - Report to Legal Expert for review
