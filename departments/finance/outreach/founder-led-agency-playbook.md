# Founder-Led Agency Outbound Playbook

**Date:** 2026-03-08
**Status:** READY

## ICP

- Founder-led B2B agencies with 5-30 people
- Priority segments:
  SEO
  paid ads
  web/dev
  automation
  RevOps
- English-speaking markets only

## Hard Filters

- B2B service business, not ecommerce
- Clear high-ticket service
- Founder visible on website or LinkedIn
- Website has contact form or book-a-call CTA
- No obvious mature outbound machine
- Not a solo freelancer
- Not a giant agency
- Ideally recent activity: founder posts, hiring, fresh case study, or new service page

## Good Contact Titles

- Founder
- Co-Founder
- Managing Director
- Head of Growth
- Sales Director

## Research Fields Per Account

- Company name
- Website
- Segment
- Employee range
- Geography
- Founder name
- Title
- Work email or verified contact path
- LinkedIn URL
- High-ticket offer
- Recent activity signal
- Outbound maturity note
- Fit score
- Personalized opening line 1
- Personalized opening line 2
- Personalized opening line 3

## Disqualifiers

- Gmail or generic personal-only contact path
- No clear premium service offer
- Ecommerce-heavy positioning
- Obvious SDR team, outbound job posts, or mature outbound system
- No founder visibility at all

## Offer Snapshot

- Promise: We build you a qualified outbound pipeline so you can spend more time closing and less time prospecting.
- Starter: $300 one-time for 100 qualified prospects, enrichment, outreach-ready handoff, and 3 personalized opening-line examples
- Growth: $500/month for weekly refreshed target lists, qualification notes, outreach iteration, and simple weekly reporting
