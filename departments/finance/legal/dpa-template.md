## 1. Purpose & Scope
This DPA governs the processing of personal data by Processor on behalf of Controller for AI-powered lead qualification services.

## 2. Definitions
- **Personal Data:** Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.
- **Processing:** Any operation performed on Personal Data (collection, storage, use, deletion).
- **Controller:** The entity that determines purposes and means of processing (Customer).
- **Processor:** The entity that processes data on behalf of Controller (Pandora Lead Qual).

## 3. Processor Obligations
Processor will:
- Process Personal Data only on documented instructions from Controller.
- Ensure personnel are bound by confidentiality obligations.
- Implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures.
- Assist Controller in responding to data subject requests.
- Delete or return all Personal Data upon termination.
- Maintain records of processing activities.
- Notify Controller of data breaches within 48 hours.

## 4. Data Subject Rights
Processor will assist Controller in fulfilling data subject requests under GDPR/CCPA:
- **Right to Access:** Provide copy of data within 30 days.
- **Right to Rectification:** Correct inaccurate data.
- **Right to Erasure:** Delete data upon request.
- **Right to Portability:** Provide data in structured, machine-readable format.
- **Right to Object:** Cease processing upon objection (unless legitimate interest overrides).

## 5. International Transfers
- Data may be transferred outside the EU/UK.
- Processor uses Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for transfers.
- Processor certifies adequate safeguards per GDPR Chapter V.

## 6. Subprocessors
Processor may engage subprocessors (e.g., cloud hosting, enrichment APIs):
- Current subprocessors listed at: [URL to subprocessor list]
- Processor notifies Controller of new subprocessors.
- Processor remains liable for subprocessor compliance.

## 7. Security Measures
Processor implements:
- **Encryption:** Data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3+) and at rest (AES-256).
- **Access Control:** Role-based access, MFA required.
- **Logging:** All access logged and monitored.
- **Backups:** Daily encrypted backups.
- **Incident Response:** Documented breach response plan.

## 8. Audit Rights
Controller may audit Processor's compliance once annually with 30 days notice.

## 9. Term & Termination
- This DPA remains in effect while Processor processes Controller's data.
- Upon termination, Processor deletes all Personal Data within 30 days.
- Deletion certified in writing upon request.

## 10. Liability
- Processor liable for damages caused by breach of this DPA.
- Liability capped at 3 months of fees ($891) unless gross negligence.

## 11. Governing Law
Governed by laws of New South Wales, Australia.
