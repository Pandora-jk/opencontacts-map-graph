# Privacy Policy - Pandora Lead Qual
**Effective Date:** March 8, 2026  
**Last Updated:** March 8, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction
Pandora Lead Qual ("we," "us," "our") provides AI-powered lead qualification services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws (GDPR, CCPA, Australian Privacy Act).

This policy explains:
- What data we collect
- How we use it
- Your rights
- How to contact us

---

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Customer Data (B2B Leads)
When you use our service, we process data from your inbound leads:
- **Company Information:** Company name, website, industry, company size
- **Contact Information:** Work email address, job title, phone number (business)
- **Behavioral Data:** Lead source (e.g., contact form, demo request), message content
- **Enrichment Data:** Employee count, revenue estimates, tech stack, funding stage (from public sources)

**Legal Basis (GDPR):** Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) for B2B communications.

### 2.2 Customer Account Data
When you sign up as a customer:
- **Business Details:** Company name, address, ABN/registration number
- **Contact Person:** Name, work email, phone number
- **Payment Data:** Processed securely by Stripe (we do not store card details)
- **Usage Data:** Service usage logs, support requests

**Legal Basis (GDPR):** Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b)).

### 2.3 What We Do NOT Collect
- ❌ Personal emails (Gmail, Yahoo, Hotmail, etc.)
- ❌ Sensitive data (health, race, religion, political views)
- ❌ Credit card numbers (handled by Stripe)
- ❌ Passwords or authentication credentials

---

## 3. How We Use Data

### 3.1 Primary Purposes
We use data to:
- Score and qualify leads on behalf of our customers
- Enrich lead data with company information
- Generate personalized email drafts
- Provide customer support
- Bill for services

### 3.2 Secondary Purposes
- **Service Improvement:** Analyze usage patterns to improve algorithms
- **Security:** Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
- **Compliance:** Meet legal obligations (tax, audit, data requests)
- **Communication:** Send service updates, invoices, important notices

**We do NOT:**
- Sell your data or our customers' lead data to third parties
- Use lead data to train models for other customers
- Share data for marketing purposes without consent

---

## 4. Data Sources

We obtain data from:
- **Customer Uploads:** Leads submitted via our service (CSV, webhook, API)
- **Public Sources:** Company websites, business registries, LinkedIn public profiles
- **Third-Party APIs:** Enrichment providers (e.g., Clearbit, Hunter)

All data is publicly available B2B information. We do not purchase email lists.

---

## 5. Data Sharing

We share data only in these circumstances:

### 5.1 Service Providers (Subprocessors)
We use trusted third parties to deliver our service:
- **Stripe:** Payment processing
- **AWS:** Cloud hosting and storage
- **Clearbit/Hunter:** Data enrichment (when configured)
- **Email Service Providers:** Sending cold emails (when configured)

All subprocessors are bound by confidentiality and data protection obligations.

### 5.2 Legal Requirements
We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or government request.

### 5.3 Business Transfers
In case of merger, acquisition, or sale, data may be transferred to the acquiring entity (with notice to customers).

**We do NOT sell data to data brokers or third-party marketers.**

---

## 6. International Data Transfers

Our service is global. Data may be transferred to:
- **Australia** (our primary operations)
- **United States** (cloud hosting via AWS/Stripe)
- **European Union** (if customer or lead is EU-based)

**Safeguards:**
- EU data transfers use Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
- All transfers encrypted (TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest)
- Subprocessors certified under Privacy Shield (if applicable)

---

## 7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security:
- **Encryption:** TLS 1.3 for data in transit; AES-256 for data at rest
- **Access Control:** Role-based access, MFA required for all staff
- **Logging:** All data access logged and monitored
- **Backups:** Daily encrypted backups
- **Testing:** Regular security audits and penetration testing

**Breach Notification:** We notify affected customers within 48 hours of discovering a breach.

## 8. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period | Deletion Method |
|-----------|------------------|-----------------|

## 9. Your Rights

You have the following rights under GDPR, CCPA, and Australian Privacy Act:

### 9.1 Right to Access
Request a copy of personal data we hold about you.

**How:** Email privacy@pandora-leadqual.com  
**Response Time:** 30 days

### 9.2 Right to Rectification
Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

**How:** Email privacy@pandora-leadqual.com  
**Response Time:** 30 days

### 9.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")
Request deletion of personal data (subject to legal exceptions).

**How:** Email privacy@pandora-leadqual.com or use unsubscribe link  
**Response Time:** 30 days

### 9.4 Right to Restriction
Request limitation of processing (e.g., while accuracy is verified).

**How:** Email privacy@pandora-leadqual.com  
**Response Time:** 30 days

### 9.5 Right to Portability
Receive data in a structured, machine-readable format.

**How:** Email privacy@pandora-leadqual.com  
**Response Time:** 30 days

### 9.6 Right to Object
Object to processing based on legitimate interest.

**How:** Use unsubscribe link in emails or email privacy@pandora-leadqual.com  
**Response Time:** Immediate (automated)

### 9.7 Right to Opt-Out (CCPA)
Opt-out of "sale" of personal information (we do not sell data).

**How:** Email privacy@pandora-leadqual.com  
**Link:** [Do Not Sell My Personal Information](#)

## 10. Cold Email Compliance

We and our customers comply with:
- **CAN-SPAM Act (USA):** Accurate headers, clear identification, opt-out mechanism
- **GDPR (EU):** Legitimate interest, relevance, opt-out
- **Spam Act 2003 (Australia):** Consent, identification, unsubscribe

**Every email includes:**
- Clear sender identification
- Physical mailing address
- Functional unsubscribe link

**Unsubscribe requests are processed within 10 business days (automated: immediate).**

## 11. Children's Privacy

Our service is for B2B use only. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18. If we discover such data, we delete it immediately.
