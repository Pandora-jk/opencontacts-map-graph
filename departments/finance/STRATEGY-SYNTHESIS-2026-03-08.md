# Strategy Synthesis: Five Review Consolidation

**Date:** 2026-03-08
**Status:** ACTIVE

## Executive Summary

Five independent LLM reviews were compared.
The shared signal was strong:

- B2B lead gen / qualification is the best primary business
- GitHub bounties are the best zero-capital secondary lane
- Skills, templates, and products should be derived later from proven demand
- Generic automation ideas, trading, and broad content plays are weak or fragile

The most detailed review was weighted more heavily because it focused on the real issue:
**revenue only lasts if there is a defensible edge.**

## What Changed

The department is no longer optimized for diversification on paper.
It is now optimized for finding one offer that real buyers will actually pay for.

## Final Recommendation

### Active
1. B2B pipeline setup service
2. GitHub bounties

### Incubating
3. Website cleanup as an upsell only

### Paused
4. Skills until they come from repeated client work
5. Data brokerage
6. Crypto
7. Standalone web design
8. Generic AI product ideation

## Why This Plan Wins

- Buyers already budget for pipeline and sales help
- The offer can be sold before heavy product build-out
- The work generates direct customer learning
- If delivery repeats, productization becomes grounded instead of speculative

## Department Rule

No more than two active lanes at once.
Anything else must be incubating or paused.

## Next 14 Days

- Pick one niche and one promise for the core service
- Build and qualify the first target list
- Run outreach with legal review in place
- Prepare demos and delivery checklist for the first client
- Use bounties as side cash only

## Productization Rule

Do not build a standalone product first.
Only package what clients repeatedly ask for after 3-5 paying customers.
