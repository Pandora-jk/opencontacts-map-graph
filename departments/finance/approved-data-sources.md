# Approved Public B2B Data Sources

**Date:** 2026-03-13
**Purpose:** Legal compliance for outbound cold email to B2B agencies

## Compliance Principles

- ✅ Only public business data (not personal emails)
- ✅ Legitimate interest basis (GDPR)
- ✅ Clear unsubscribe mechanism
- ✅ No scraped data from unknown sources
- ✅ Verified contact paths (LinkedIn, company websites)

## Approved Data Sources

### 1. LinkedIn Company Pages
- **Data Available:** Company size, industry, location, website
- **Contact Access:** Public employee listings, leadership pages
- **Compliance:** Opt-in via profile visibility settings
- **Limitations:** Cannot directly scrape personal emails (violation of Terms of Service)

### 2. Company Websites
- **Data Available:** Company name, website, segment, contact form
- **Contact Access:** Public contact pages, "Book a Call" CTAs
- **Compliance:** Public information, no scraping required
- **Limitations:** May require manual extraction

### 3. LinkedIn Sales Navigator (Optional - Paid)
- **Data Available:** Enhanced company info, verified decision-maker titles
- **Contact Access:** Direct LinkedIn messaging (opt-in)
- **Compliance:** Paid license, legitimate business use
- **Limitations:** Cost per month

### 4. Industry Directories (Verified)
- **Data Available:** Agency listings, certifications, locations
- **Contact Access:** Public contact information where available
- **Compliance:** Public directories, verified listings
- **Limitations:** May have outdated information

### 5. Conference & Event Attendees (Verified)
- **Data Available:** Company name, attendee type, event details
- **Contact Access:** Public speaker/attendee lists
- **Compliance:** Opt-in via conference registration
- **Limitations:** Event-specific, limited frequency

## Data Sources to AVOID (Illegal/Compliance Risk)

### ❌ Scraped Email Lists
- Scraped from websites (violates Terms of Service)
- Purchased from unknown brokers
- Auto-enriched tools without disclosure

### ❌ Personal Data Collection
- Personal emails (even if business-related)
- Phone numbers without explicit consent
- Social media DMs without prior interaction

### ❌ Purchased Data from Unknown Sources
- No verification of data quality
- Unknown source of data collection
- High risk of GDPR/CCPA violations

## Contact Verification Process

For every prospect in the 20-company target list:

1. **Verify Company Website:** Check for agency services, client list, testimonials
2. **Verify LinkedIn Presence:** Confirm founder visibility, recent activity
3. **Verify Contact Title:** Confirm role matches decision-maker (founder, head of growth, sales director)
4. **Verify Contact Path:** Prefer LinkedIn message or company contact form over direct email
5. **Verify Fit:** Confirm company size (5-30 employees), segment (SEO/paid ads/web/dev/automation/RevOps), English-speaking market
